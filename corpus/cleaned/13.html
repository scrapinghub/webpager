Amsterdam, Netherlands Dining Guide

With a restaurant scene as varied and colorful as the city itself, Amsterdam, Netherlands restaurants offer near-endless pleasures. Savor everything from traditional Continental elegance at Michelin star-rated restaurants to tr adtional hearty Netherlands classics and the innovative flavors of the "New Dutch Cuisine" movement, which takes its inspiration from around the world.

MOMO

MOMO Restaurant, bar and lounge blends the flavors and colours of the Far East with the style and sophistication of the West, bringing an exciting new concept to Amsterdam. Located at Park Hotel Amsterdam, enjoy enticing food and drinks from around the world in a sexy interior with funky music, energy and style. To learn more about MOMO, visit parkhotel.warwickhotels.com

De Kas

De Kas is set in a 1926 greenhouse, where chefs pick the produce that goes into the Mediterranean-themed dishes that make are served on the plate. The light-filled space is a delightful backdrop for the ever-changing prix-fixe menu. To learn more about this popular Amsterdam restaurant, visit www.restaurantdekas.nl

Ron Blaauw

The Ron Blaauw is a one star Michelin restaurant in Amsterdam set in the lovely Amstel river area. Set in a modern dining room, the menu features French cuisine that is seasonally based and locally sourced. To learn more about one of Amsterdam's highest rated restaurants, visit www.ronblaauw.nl

Le Garage

Le Garage is a charming brasserie set in a former garage that is known as a place to see and be seen. Helmed by well-known TV chef Joop Braakhekke, the well prepared food offers the finest French haute cuisine and contains many foie gras dishes. To learn more, visit www.restaurantlegarage.nl

Blauw aan de Wal

Blauw aan de Wal is located on a small alley in Amsterdam's famed Red Light District. "Blue on the Quay," as it is known in English, is set in the courtyard of a former monastery and still possesses its tranquil nature. The modern Mediterranean menu is innovative and uses the freshest ingredients. To learn more, visit www.blauwaandewal.com

Envy

Envy is a seductive new style of eatery in Amsterdam that offers soft lighting and an inspired menu of unique combinations. Featuring appetizer-size menu items, this stylish Amsterdam restaurant has an open kitchen that offers glimpses of the the chefs preparing their creations. To learn more, visit www.envy.nl
